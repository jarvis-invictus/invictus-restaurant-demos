Our Story

A Love Letter to Indian Cuisine

Spice Route was born from a simple belief: that Indian food, at its best, is among the most sophisticated and diverse cuisines in the world. We're not a buffet. We're not "curry house." We are a fine dining experience dedicated to the depth and beauty of regional Indian cooking.

Chef Arjun Mehta spent 11 years travelling across India — apprenticing under a Lucknowi khansama, learning coastal fish curries in Mangalore, mastering dum cooking in Hyderabad. Every dish on our menu carries that story.
