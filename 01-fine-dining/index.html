Our Story
A Kitchen Built on Tradition

Spice Route Kitchen started as a 12-table dhaba in Koregaon Park in 2012. Today we serve 200+ guests daily, cater events across Pune, and deliver to over 15 neighbourhoods — but the recipes haven't changed.

Our head chef, Anwar Khan, trained in the kitchens of the Taj Rambagh Palace, Jaipur. Every dish carries that lineage.
