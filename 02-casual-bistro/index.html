Why Us

Why Pune Keeps Coming Back

Three things make us different. Three things we'll never change.

🏗️

Open Since 2003

Over 20 years of feeding Pune. Started with one tandoor and a dream. Now we're a Shivajinagar institution — same spot, same family, same fire.

📜

Original Amritsari Recipes

Our recipes came in a trunk from Amritsar. Biji's dal makhani. Papaji's tandoori masala. We didn't learn this from YouTube — it's in the bloodline.

🧈

No Cutting Corners on Ghee

You'll see the ghee floating. That's not a mistake — that's the point. Real makkhan, real malai, real Punjabi portions. Your diet can wait.
