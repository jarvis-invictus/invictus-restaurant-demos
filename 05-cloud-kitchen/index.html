15 Years of Dum

A family recipe. A Pune institution.

Biryani House was founded in 2009 by Imran Siddiqui, whose family brought the original Hyderabadi dum recipe from the kitchens of the Nizam. Every batch is made the same way it was made in 1985.

We use only aged Basmati (minimum 2 years), fresh spices ground every morning, and genuine saffron imported from Kashmir. There are no shortcuts. There never will be.

12-Hour Marination

Aged Basmati Selection

Sealed Dum Cooking

Pot sealed with dough, cooked on dum (low heat) for 3 hours. Steam stays in, flavour concentrates.

Saffron Finishing

Everything you need to know about Biryani House

What types of biryani does Biryani House serve in Pune? +
Biryani House serves 12 varieties of authentic dum biryani — Chicken, Mutton, Veg, Egg, Prawn, and more in Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, and Kolkata styles. All biryanis are slow-cooked for 3 hours with 12-hour marinated meat.
What makes Biryani House biryani special compared to others? +
Every biryani is slow-cooked (dum) for 3 hours in sealed handis using 2-year aged basmati rice, hand-ground spices, genuine Kashmir saffron, and 12-hour marinated meat. No shortcuts, no pre-cooking — authentic dum since 2009.
