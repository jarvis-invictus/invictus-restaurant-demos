Our Story

A Little Piece of
Karnataka in Pune

What started as a small 10-seater eatery in 2009 has grown into Karve Nagar's favourite South Indian spot. Our founder, Srinivas Rao, brought authentic recipes from his hometown in Udupi — the same fermented batter, the same hand-ground chutneys, the same love on every plate.

We believe food should be honest. No shortcuts, no MSG, no frozen ingredients. Our sambar powder is stone-ground in-house. Our coconut chutney is made fresh every two hours. And our filter coffee? Let's just say people drive across Pune for it.
