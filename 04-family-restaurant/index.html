FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Café Milano

What type of cuisine does Café Milano serve in Koregaon Park, Pune? +
Café Milano serves authentic Italian cuisine — wood-fired Neapolitan pizzas at 450°C, handmade fresh pasta daily, risottos, antipasti, tiramisu, and classic Italian dishes. All pasta is made in-house.
Does Café Milano have a wine menu and bar in Pune? +
Yes, Café Milano curates an Italian wine cellar with 70+ labels from Tuscany, Piedmont, and Sicily — including Chianti, Prosecco, Pinot Grigio — alongside cocktails, craft beers, and Italian sodas. Sommelier pairings available.
Is Café Milano suitable for private dining and special events? +
Yes, Café Milano has a private dining room for up to 12 guests. Perfect for anniversaries, proposals, corporate dinners, and birthday celebrations with customised Italian menus and dedicated service.
How much does a meal cost at Café Milano Pune? +
A meal for two costs approximately ₹1,200-₹3,000 for food. Pizzas range ₹350-₹650, pasta ₹300-₹550, mains ₹450-₹800. Sunday brunch is available 11 AM-4 PM. Alcohol billed separately.
Where is Café Milano located and what are the hours? +
Café Milano is at Lane 5, Koregaon Park, Pune 411001. Lunch: 12-3:30 PM, Dinner: 7-11:30 PM, Sunday Brunch: 11 AM-4 PM. Closed Mondays. Live jazz every Friday at 8 PM. Valet parking available.
